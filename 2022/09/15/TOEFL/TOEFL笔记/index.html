TOEFL笔记

发表于2022-09-15|更新于2022-10-15
|阅读量:

Vocabulary

sweep to power 上台执政
lecture in/on sth 讲授
lecture sb (about sth)指责，训斥，告诫
aristocrat贵族
manipulate控制，操纵，影响；操作，使用
sap

n.（植物体内运送养分的）液，汁; 笨蛋，易上当的人

v.使虚弱；削弱；逐渐破坏
•The hot sun sapped our energy. 火辣辣的太阳烤得我们虚软无力。

•Years of failure have sapped him of his confidence. 连年失败使他逐渐丧失了自信。

speculation

推测；猜测；推断

•There was widespread speculation that she was going to resign. 人们纷纷推测她将辞职。

•His private life is the subject of much speculation. 他的私生活引起诸多猜测。

•Today’s announcement ends months of speculation about the company’s future. 今天的声明使得几个月来关于公司未来的种种猜测就此烟消云散。

•She dismissed the newspaper reports as pure speculation . 她说报纸上的报道毫无根据，纯属臆断。

•Our speculations proved right. 事实证明，我们的推断是对的

投机买卖；炒股

agrarian 农业的；土地的；耕地的
envision 展望；想象

•They envision an equal society, free of poverty and disease. 他们向往一个没有贫穷和疾病的平等社会。

as a rule 通常，一般说来

philosophy

n.哲学

n.哲学体系；思想体系

n.人生哲学；生活的信条（或态度）

**•**Her philosophy of life is to take every opportunity that presents itself. 她的处世态度是不放过任何呈现眼前的机会。

sphere

1.( geometry 几何) a solid figure that is completely round, with every point on its surface at an equal distance from the centre 球；球体；球形

2.any object that is completely round, for example a ball 圆球；球状物

3.an area of activity, influence or interest; a particular section of society 范围；领域；阶层；界

**•**the political sphere 政界

**•**This area was formerly within the sphere of influence of the US. 这一地区先前属于美国的势力范围。

**•**He and I moved in totally different social spheres. 我和他处在完全不同的社会圈子里。

4.-sphere* ( in nouns 构成名词 ) a region that surrounds a planet, especially the earth （包围地球等的大气的）层

**•**ionosphere 电离层

**•**atmosphere 大气层

domain 领域，领土

dominant adj.占支配地位 dominate v.统治，支配

domestic adj.国内的，家庭的 domesticate v.驯养,驯化

perishable adj

( especially of food 尤指食物 ) likely to decay or go bad quickly 易腐烂的；易变质的

• perishable goods/foods 易腐烂变质的商品╱食物

perish v

1.[ V] ( formal literary) ( of people or animals 人或动物 ) to die, especially in a sudden violent way 死亡；暴死

**•**A family of four perished in the fire. 一家四口死于此次火灾之中。

2.[ V] ( formal ) to be lost or destroyed 丧失；湮灭；毁灭

**•**Early buildings were made of wood and have perished. 早期建筑物为木质结构，已经消失殆尽。

3.[ V**VN] ( BrE ) if a material such as rubber perishes or is perished , it becomes damaged, weaker or full of holes （使橡胶等）老化，脆裂

convention n

1.习俗，惯例

social conventions

By convention the deputy leader was always a woman. 按惯例，这一领导职务的副职总是由女性担任。

2.大会，集会

to hold a convention 召开大会

3.公约，协定，协议

the Geneva convention 日内瓦公约

primate n.首领，大主教，灵长类动物

primary adj 1.主要的；最重要的；基本的 2.最初的；最早的 3.初等教育的；小学教育的 n（美国）初选

primarily adv 主要地；根本地

prime

adj 1.主要的；首要的；基本的 2.优质的；上乘的；优异的 3典型的；有代表性的 4最可能的；首选的；最适宜的

n 盛年；年富力强的时期；鼎盛时期

**•**a young woman in her prime 正当妙龄的女郎

**•**He was barely 30 and in the prime of (his) life . 他还不满30岁，正是英姿勃发的年华。

**•**These flowers are long past their prime . 这些花的鼎盛花期早过了。

v.事先指点；使（某人）做好准备

They had been primed with good advice. 他们事先得到了高人指点

primitive adj 1原始的；远古的 2发展水平低的；落后的 3原始本能的

a primitive instinct 原始本能

primitive tribes 原始部落

primitive beliefs 原始的信仰

resident

n.居民；住户；住宿者，旅客，房客

**•**a resident of the United States 美国的居民

adj.living in a particular place （在某地）居住的

**•**the town’s resident population (= not tourists or visitors) 镇上的居民

**•**to be resident abroad/in the US 常驻国外╱美国

**•**Tom’s our resident expert (= our own expert) on foreign movies. 汤姆是我们自己的外国电影专家。

residence

[ C] a house, especially a large or impressive one 住所；住房；（尤指）宅第，豪宅

**•**a desirable family residence for sale (= for example, in an advertisement) 待售的理想家居

**•**10 Downing Street is the British Prime Minister’s official residence . 唐宁街10号是英国首相的官邸。

[ U] the state of living in a particular place 居住；定居

**•**They were not able to take up residence in their new home until the spring. 他们到第二年春天才住进了新家。

**•**Please state your occupation and place of residence . 请说明你的职业和住址。

**•**The flag flies when the Queen is in residence . 女王的王宫及驻跸处均有国旗飘扬。

( also resi·dency ) [ U] permission to live in a country that is not your own （在他国的）居住权，居留许可

**•**They have been denied residence in this country. 这个国家不给他们居住资格。

**•**a residence permit 居留许可证
